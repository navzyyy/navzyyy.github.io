The Club

Founder: Navin Jayden Nair

Founded: 22-06-2022

Founder's Profile

Read time: 2 minutes

50 Boyz started as a small, tight-knit group of friends who bonded over countless hours of playing FIFA. We weren't just playing a video game; we were sharing passion, debating tactics, and building our dream teams. These virtual matches quickly became more than just a pastime. The camaraderie and competitive spirit we experienced through FIFA inspired us to take the game from the screen to the streets.

"We'll build this club brick by brick" - Franklin, Chairman

What began as weekend football sessions in the park grew into something bigger. We met regularly, turning casual kickabouts into more organized matches. The love for the game kept us pushing each other to improve, and soon, we realized that the passion we shared could turn into something serious. This is where the dream of 50 Boyz as a football club took shape.

Starting this club wasn’t just about playing football—it was about translating the essence of what we loved about FIFA and our park matches into real life. It was about building a community of like-minded people who love the game as much as we do. From those early days of casual park football, 50 Boyz has now evolved into a full-fledged football club, aiming for professional success while never forgetting our roots.

The name “50 Boyz” stands as a tribute to the original squad of friends, and it represents more than just football. It’s about unity, friendship, and the belief that big dreams can come from humble beginnings. Our journey from FIFA enthusiasts to a professional club is proof that passion, persistence, and teamwork can take you anywhere.

Our Journey So Far

Read time: 1 minutes

We’ve grown from a group of friends playing in the park to a fully organized football club. We’ve built a strong administrative team, established our club’s infrastructure, and put in place the necessary systems for operations and player development.

Our dedication on the field has paid off. We’ve participated in several local and regional tournaments, consistently showing strong performances and earning respect in the football community. Our focus on skill development and teamwork has begun to translate into notable victories and commendable standings.

"Once you taste the game, you never lose that sensation" - Khan Khan, Assistant Manager

We've made significant strides in community engagement. Our outreach programs and local initiatives have not only brought us closer to our fans but have also made a positive impact in the areas we serve. We've hosted youth clinics, charity events, and community matches that have strengthened our connection with the local community.

Our commitment to nurturing talent has resulted in the growth of several standout players. We've developed a structured training program that helps players improve their skills and reach their potential. Many of our players have shown impressive development and are making their mark both in our matches and beyond.

The support from our fans has been overwhelming. Our matches are now well-attended, and our social media presence has grown significantly. We're excited to see our community of supporters expand and engage with the club in new and meaningful ways.

We've secured valuable partnerships and sponsorships that have provided us with additional resources and opportunities. These relationships are crucial for our continued growth and success, helping us to enhance our facilities, support our players, and reach new heights.

Our Vision

Read time: 2 minutes

Our primary goal is to establish ourselves as a competitive force in the world of football. We aim to build a team that not only plays with skill and determination but also embodies the values of hard work, sportsmanship, and unity. Every player who wears the 50 Boyz badge carries the spirit of the club's beginnings—a reminder that success comes from dedication, teamwork, and passion for the game.

But we're not just focused on the pitch. We're deeply committed to fostering a sense of community, much like the one that started our journey. One of our key objectives is to develop youth talent by providing opportunities for young players to grow and thrive, ensuring that the next generation of footballers can follow a similar path from grassroots football to the professional stage.

"I want us to get the respect we deserve, we'll win together and we'll lose together" - Singh, Captain

In addition, we want 50 Boyz to be a symbol of inclusivity and diversity. Football is a universal language, and we aim to bring together people from all walks of life, creating a supportive environment where everyone can participate and contribute to the club's success.

Ultimately, our goal is to inspire others to chase their dreams, just as we did. Whether it's on the field, in the community, or through personal development, 50 Boyz is about more than just football; it's about turning passion into progress and dreams into reality.
